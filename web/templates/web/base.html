The Central Jersey Transportation Forum (CJTF) was formed in 1999 to coordinate planning among municipal and county leaders at the border of the DVRPC and NJTPA metropolitan planning organizations, with the goals of managing growth and increasing mobility throughout the Central Jersey area.
